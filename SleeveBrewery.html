Building the site

After a hectic week in Boone with the season's first snow and tons of schoolwork to be done, the site is finally beginning to take shape. The Sleeve Brewing Co. has been busy as well. In the last two weeks, a big Double/Imperial IPA has been brewed, along with an American Bitter and a RyePA. More than anything, this blog is just to keep people informed about what is going on at the Sleeve and what we are going to have in store for you in the future.

Cheers,
-The Sleeve
